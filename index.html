Welcome to Dar Alsalam

 

Dar Alsalam is a human service center in the Middle East, located in Syria - Sweida city with some branches in different countries.

It is run by Dr. Nawwaf Alshibli who is a well known spiritual master and philanthropist along with a group of dedicated devotees and sevadals.
